Survival 4
12.05.2006 - 20.05.2006

Wrocław MAIN RAILWAY STATION as an exhibition space
“Survival” is a festival presenting “young art” (“młoda sztuka”) outside of gallery and other exhibition space. This year, its 4th edition takes the city’s main railway station as a host and presents the works and performance by 50 artists and artist groups at the various locations inside the station building.

Programme:
12.05 & 13.05.2006, 15:00-20:00 Performance and video projections
14.05.2006, 15:00-20:00 Some of the performance and video projections
15.05-20.05.2006 Installation and selected few video projections only
[View the photo gallery]

Interview: Modern Art & Public Space

 
The 4th edition of the “Survival” art festival is held, for the first time in its history, in the open public space: the main railway station of Wrocław. One of the key organizers – Michał Bieniek – goes so far as to describe it as “the public space per excellence,” the space that the travellers, the homeless people, the poor and the rich all share alike. In our video interview, we asked Michał Bieniek why “Survival” – why this title was given to this festival and what is its necessity today (political, artistic or otherwise). We also asked him about the experience of working with the space of the railway station.
