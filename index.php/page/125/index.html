La Luz !

>>> Podcasts | Posted by toyo on February 7th, 2006

La Luz is a newly opened international café & winebar in the wine-tower from the 13th century. Many of the locals have known this wine-tower as “Baszta” for many years.

Conveniently located between Rynek and Ostrów Tumski, the fine art academy and the university main building, and also between the Radisson Hotel and the city center, La Luz offers an ideal location for relaxation. Its specialities are the collection of affordable wine, authentic (as opposed to “Polish-style”) Mexican dishes, the eclectic collection of music (world music, jazz, reggae, blues, Cuban, salsa, tango, ambiance etc.), and, finally, the moody lightings, all of which are carefully arranged by Frederick Abrams, its owner and the multi-media artist.

In our interview, Frederick Abrams talks about how it all started and where it is heading.

Stay Fit!

>>> Because We Care | Posted by toyo on February 6th, 2006

THE PERSONAL TRAINER
for the personalized physical training and exercise - by appointment
(When contacting, please quote “Wrocław Weekly”)
contact: +48 889 263 821

1 session of traning (60 - 90 min) - 50zł (+ costs of public transport and the use of exercise facility, when necessary)
This includes:
/ Consultation
/ General dietary recommendation
/ Planning of the workout routines and schedule for the next 7 days
/ Help for the workout (checking and correcting)
/ Monitoring the progress in workout (necessary for planning the following week)
/ Help for stretching after exercise
Minimum 1 session/week and maximum 3 sessions/week

In Wrocław, there are many gyms with so-called “trainers.” When these “trainers” are supposed to be helpful, many of them ignore your need and impose on you what they think good (often too much focused on heavy weight-lifting routines).
This personal training is to help you doing the exercise that you - and your body - need, according to your physical condition. The personal trainer will accompany you to the gym, swimming pool or outdoor, for the exercise that your body needs. If you have no time to travel around the city, it is also possible to arrange the training in your own living-room.

With request, the trainer can also give you a massage to relieve the stress on your neck and back. The massage session can be arranged with or without the exercise session, but it is always recommended to do exercise to keep yourself fit!

1 session of physiotherapeutic massage (45 min) - 50zł
Types of available massage:
/ Swedish massage
/ sports massage
/ slim massage
/ reflexological face massage
/ Indian head massage

The trainer profile
Finished study at Wrocław Physical Education Academy (Akademia Wychowania Fizycznego) and worked as a personal trainer in London; also completed the course of Aromatherapy.
