Forum of Possibility 2009

>>> Podcasts | Posted by toyo on January 15th, 2010



The ARKA Theatre in Wrocław regularly organizes theatre workshops for people with intellectual disabilities or mental illness for the purpose of social and professional rehabilitation. Their public-funded project “Forum Możliwości (Forum of Possibility)” aims to extend their service to other cities in Lower Silesia, and in 2009, they organized the 2nd edition of the project.

From July through November 2009, we followed their workshops held in various cities of Lower Silesia and documented the project in video. During the documentation, we interviewed several people working daily with those with disabilities or illness and asked them about the benefit of theatre workshop as a method of rehabilitation.

Digit-All-love Soundsystem at Mleczarnia

>>> Podcasts | Posted by toyo on January 4th, 2010



11.11.2009 at MLECZARNIA (ul. Włodkowica 5) - Digit-All-love is a Wrocław-based trip-hop band, and “Digit-All-love Soundsystem” is its chamber version, taking away the live drum, guitar and bass, and keeping only the vocal, strings and electronic sounds. This video features a new track from their upcoming 2nd album.

With: Natalia Grosiak, Olga Kończak, Maciek Zakrzewski, and Magda Olearczyk & Wojtek Bolsewicz. For more info on Digit-All-love can be found at: myspace.com/digitalllove
