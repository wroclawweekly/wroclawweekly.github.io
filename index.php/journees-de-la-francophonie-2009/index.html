Journées de la Francophonie 2009

>>> News

06.03.2009-22.03.2009
To commemorate the establishment of the intergovernmental organization of “Francophones,” every year around the 20th of March, SCAC (Service de coopération et d’action culturelle) in Wrocław organizes “Journées de la Francophonie” festival. The guiding theme of the festival is thus “Francophonie” – it is not only about France and French culture, but it also covers the culture, history and arts of French-speaking countries.
