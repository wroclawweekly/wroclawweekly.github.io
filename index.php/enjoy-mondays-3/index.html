Enjoy Mondays 3

>>> News

06.10.2008 - 22.12.2008 (On Selected Mondays)
“Enjoy Mondays” (“Polubisz Poniedziałki”) is a mini-festival around classical music. This 3rd edition follows the same programming principle as the previous editions: each concert with a music from a different period and a different style. As a whole, the series of concerts forms an interesting quick tour of classical music.

TICKETS: films 7 PLN, concerts 16 PLN normal/13 PLN reduced (the ticket for the following concert will be 1 PLN less with the presentation of the ticket from the previous concert)
