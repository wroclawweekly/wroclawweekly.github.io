December Events

04 Dec 2020

 9.12 /wed/ - 19:00

“History of Anime” 

In this presentation, dr Toyotaka Ota will explain what Japanese "Anime" is by tracing its history and show how it is different from other forms of animations.


18.12 /fri/ - 19:00

“An Evening with Gundam in Yokohama”

Japan will open the exhibition of a full-action, 18m-tall robot “Gundam” to the general public on 19.12 [https://gundam-challenge.com/en/]. On the eve of its official launch, dr Ota organizes an evening to watch selected making-of videos of this “Gundam” and discuss Japanese robotics and other related topics.

The evening will start with the presentation "Robots in Japan" [45min] and continue to the discussion of the real-size Gundam.[the latest press preview of Gundam: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NH7KQua_GqA]


Format: Zoom /audio only/ + a web browser

Place: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1D45FD-yUq3g3Umm72hbscwfPixnYSUTDFxoL-gV2mEk/edit?usp=sharing

All events are in English.

No registration is required for the participation. Bring your friends!
