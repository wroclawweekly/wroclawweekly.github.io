Mystical power of Japanese language

18 Oct 2021

Here’s an upcoming presentation on Japanese language/culture:

Mystical power of Japanese language 
(Mistyczna moc języka japońskiego)


date: 31.03.2022 (Thursday)
time: 18:55-20:30 (The presentation will begin at 19:00)
language: English
place: https://bit.ly/jpSJO-1 (Google doc)


This is an online presentation on Japanese language and its history.
In this presentation, I will explain what “language” means to Japanese people, and how their belief affects its everyday usage.

For the presentation, the following tools/services will be used:

  • Zoom /audio only/
  • Vimeo for video playback
  • A Google Doc (or Google Slide) as a virtual whiteboard.

This is an open presentation. Feel free to invite your friends.
