About Absence

23 Feb 2021

I’m getting a few e-mails about absence. I’d like to remind everyone that:

  1. students have the obligation to attend the lecture;
  2. students have the right to miss 2 lessons for the 45-hour course, 3 lessons for the 60-hour course, without excuse;
  3. if you are ill or have to go away because of study-related business, with the presentation of the proof, your absence will be excused;
  4. other reasons do not constitute the reason for your absence;
  5. even with excuse, the number of your absence must not exceed 10.

When you are absent because of your work or some urgent personal matter, I said I will consider the circumstance of your absence, BUT it will be still counted as “absence.” Please remember the item (1) above.
